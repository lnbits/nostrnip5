Pricing strategies
The base price applies to every identity. On top of that you can charge premium rates for short or popular names, and offer promotions. Combine any of the three strategies below.
  1. Start with the base price from the domain basics above.
  2. Check if the name matches any length bracket. If so, compare its price against the base — keep whichever is higher.
  3. Check if the name matches any popularity tier. Compare its price against the running total — keep whichever is higher.
  4. Multiply by the number of years the buyer selected.
  5. Apply any promo code discount on top.
In short: the highest-matching rate wins, then discounts apply. Use the live preview on the right to experiment.
